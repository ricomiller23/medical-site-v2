Drug

Darzalex

Anti-CD38 monoclonal antibody produced in Chinese Hamster Ovary (CHO) cells. Targets and destroys myeloma cells through complement-dependent cytotoxicity (CDC), antibody-dependent cellular cytotoxicity (ADCC), and antibody-dependent cellular phagocytosis (ADCP).

CDC

Complement-Dependent Cytotoxicity — Activates complement cascade to directly lyse myeloma cells.

ADCC

Antibody-Dependent Cellular Cytotoxicity — Recruits NK cells to destroy antibody-tagged cancer cells.

ADCP

Antibody-Dependent Cellular Phagocytosis — Macrophages engulf and digest marked myeloma cells.
